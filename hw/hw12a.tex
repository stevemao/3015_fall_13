\section{Trees}
\exercise{
Read chapter 6 of the Bird text.}


Recall the {\tt{BTree}} datatype and it's structural induction principle for finite {\tt{BTree}}s.


\begin{program*}
\>  data  BTree a = Leaf a | Fork  (BTree a) (BTree a) \\
\>   deriving (Show,Eq) 
\end{program*}
\[\begin{array}{l}

( \forall{}x:a.\;\;P[{\tt{Leaf}}\; x] \;\;\;\wedge \\
\;\;\;\;{}\;\; \forall x:a.\;\; \forall t_1,t_2: ({\tt{BTree}}\, a).\;\; (P[t_1]\;\wedge{}\;P[t_2])\Rightarrow P[{\tt{Fork}}(t_1,yt_2)])\\
\;\; \Rightarrow\\
\forall t:({\tt{BTree}}\; a).\; P[t]
\end{array}\]
